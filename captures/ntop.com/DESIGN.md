## Overview

The nTop website presents a modern, high-contrast visual design that emphasizes clarity, engineering precision, and a forward-thinking aesthetic. The design leverages a stark black and white palette, punctuated by a vibrant blue accent, to create a professional and impactful user experience. Typography, primarily using the Graphik typeface, plays a central role in establishing hierarchy and conveying information, with generous line heights and considered letter spacing. Layouts are structured and spacious, utilizing a consistent spacing scale to define clear content blocks and maintain visual breathing room. The overall impression is one of sophisticated simplicity, designed to highlight complex technical content without visual clutter.

**Key Characteristics:**
*   **High Contrast:** Dominant use of black and white for backgrounds and text.
*   **Modern Typography:** Exclusive use of the Graphik typeface for all text elements.
*   **Vibrant Accent:** A distinct bright blue (`#248aff`) is used sparingly for interactive elements and highlights.
*   **Generous Whitespace:** Ample padding and margins create a clean, uncluttered interface.
*   **Structured Layouts:** Content is organized into clear, often full-width, sections.
*   **Minimalist Aesthetic:** Absence of complex shadows or gradients, focusing on flat design principles.
*   **Rounded Elements:** Buttons feature fully rounded corners for a softer, approachable feel.
*   **Subtle Animation:** Marquee and carousel elements introduce dynamic movement without being distracting.

## Colors

**Brand & Accent**
*   **Accent Primary** (`{colors.accent-primary}` — #248aff): Used for interactive elements such as button borders, text links, and potentially hover states. It provides the primary visual highlight against the monochrome palette.

**Surface**
*   **Canvas** (`{colors.canvas}` — #ffffff): The primary background color for most content sections, providing a clean, bright base.
*   **Surface Dark** (`{colors.surface-dark}` — #000000): Used for backgrounds in sections like the footer and potentially hero areas, creating a strong contrast and sense of depth.

**Text**
*   **Text Primary** (`{colors.text-primary}` — #000000): Default text color, used on light backgrounds for maximum readability.
*   **Text On Dark** (`{colors.text-on-dark}` — #ffffff): Text color used on dark backgrounds, maintaining high contrast.
*   **Text Muted On Dark** (`{colors.text-muted-on-dark}` — rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.75)): A slightly desaturated white used for secondary text or body copy on dark backgrounds, offering a softer visual presence.

## Typography

### Font Family
The primary typeface used across the nTop website is "Graphik", with a robust fallback stack: `__Graphik_e45cba, __Graphik_Fallback_e45cba, Graphik, "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`. This modern sans-serif typeface is used for all headings, body text, and interactive elements, ensuring a consistent and professional brand voice.
