## Overview

The CRED website presents a sophisticated and exclusive brand identity, characterized by a stark dark mode aesthetic, generous whitespace, and a strong emphasis on typography. The visual design leverages a minimalist color palette, primarily black and white, to create a sense of premium quality and focus on content. Large, impactful display typography is used to convey key messages, often with wide letter spacing, while body text maintains high readability. The overall impression is one of modern elegance and understated luxury, reinforcing the brand's "not everyone gets it" tagline.

**Key Characteristics:**
*   **Dominant Dark Theme**: Primarily black backgrounds with white text.
*   **Generous Whitespace**: Ample padding and margins create breathing room around content.
*   **Bold Typography**: Large, distinctive display fonts like "Denton" and "Gilroy" command attention.
*   **High Contrast**: White text on black backgrounds ensures excellent readability.
*   **Minimalist Aesthetic**: Focus on essential elements, avoiding clutter.
*   **Sharp Edges**: Absence of significant border-radius contributes to a crisp, modern feel.
*   **Subtle Depth**: Shadows are used sparingly, primarily for functional elements or subtle lifts.
*   **Structured Layouts**: Content is often presented in distinct, well-padded sections.

## Colors

**Surface**
*   **Canvas** (`{colors.canvas}` — #000000): The primary background color for the entire site, establishing the dark theme.
*   **Surface Dark** (`{colors.surface-dark}` — #111213): A very dark gray, used for subtle variations in dark backgrounds or elements.
*   **Surface Light** (`{colors.surface-light}` — #f1f3f9): A very light gray, likely used for light-themed components or backgrounds in specific contexts.
*   **Surface Light Alt** (`{colors.surface-light-alt}` — #f8f9fc): An off-white, similar to `surface-light`, for light-themed elements.
*   **Transparent** (`{colors.transparent}` — rgba(255, 255, 255, 0)): Fully transparent, used for backgrounds of certain cards or overlays.

**Text**
*   **On Canvas** (`{colors.on-canvas}` — #ffffff): The primary text color, used extensively on dark backgrounds for high contrast.
*   **Text Secondary** (`{colors.text-secondary}` — #333333): A dark gray for secondary text, likely used on lighter backgrounds or for less prominent information.
*   **Text Muted Dark** (`{colors.text-muted-dark}` — #0d0d0d): A very dark gray, almost black, for subtle text on light backgrounds.
*   **Text Muted Light** (`{colors.text-muted-light}` — #5e636e): A medium gray for muted text on lighter backgrounds.
*   **Link** (`{colors.link}` — #0000ee): A standard blue for interactive text links.

**Hairlines & Borders**
*   **Border Dark** (`{colors.border-dark}` — #000000): Black, used for borders on light elements.
*   **Border Light** (`{colors.border-light}` — #ffffff): White, used for borders on dark elements.
*   **Border Muted Light** (`{colors.border-muted-light}` — #c2c9d6): A light gray for subtle borders.
*   **Border Default Light** (`{colors.border-default-light}` — #dee3ed): A slightly darker light gray for default borders.
*   **Border Translucent Light** (`{colors.border-translucent-light}` — rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.2)): A semi-transparent white, used for subtle borders on dark backgrounds, particularly for cards.

**Overlay**
*   **Overlay Subtle** (`{colors.overlay-subtle}` — rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.04)): A very subtle transparent black, used for light overlays.
*   **Overlay Default** (`{colors.overlay-default}` — rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1)): A slightly more prominent transparent black, for overlays.

**Semantic**
*   **Accent Green** (`{colors.accent-green}` — rgba(23, 135, 51, 0.8)): A green color, likely used for call-to-action pulses or success indicators.

## Typography

### Font Family
The primary typefaces used are "Gilroy" and "Denton". "Gilroy" (including `gilroy-regular`, `gilroy-medium`, `gilroy-bold`) serves as the main sans-serif for body text, UI elements, and some headings, offering a modern and clean aesthetic. "Denton" is a distinctive display serif typeface, used for large, impactful headlines, providing a unique and sophisticated brand voice. "Overpass-SemiBold" appears in a few instances for specific display text.
