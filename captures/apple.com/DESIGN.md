## Overview

The Apple iPad website presents a clean, sophisticated, and highly structured visual design, characteristic of the Apple brand. It emphasizes clarity, generous whitespace, and a precise typographic hierarchy to guide the user through product information. The design relies on a minimalist color palette, primarily shades of dark gray and white, with subtle accents for interactive elements. Imagery plays a crucial role, often serving as the primary visual interest against neutral backgrounds. The overall impression is one of premium quality, ease of use, and meticulous attention to detail, reflecting a strong brand identity.

**Key Characteristics:**
*   **Minimalist Color Palette**: Dominated by dark grays, black, and white, with a single vibrant blue for focus states.
*   **Precise Typography**: Extensive use of SF Pro Text and SF Pro Display, with fine-tuned letter spacing and line heights.
*   **Generous Whitespace**: Ample padding and margins create breathing room around content, enhancing readability and visual calm.
*   **Sharp Edges**: Predominantly uses sharp, unrounded corners for most UI elements, with a notable exception for a larger `--corner-radius` variable.
*   **Flat Design**: Absence of shadows or complex gradients, contributing to a modern, flat aesthetic.
*   **Content-First Layout**: Layouts are designed to highlight product imagery and concise textual information.
*   **Subtle Interactivity**: Focus states are clearly defined but not overly flashy, maintaining elegance.

## Colors

### Brand & Accent
*   **Interactive Focus** (`{colors.interactive-focus}` — #0071e3): Used for interactive elements' focus states, providing a clear visual cue without disrupting the minimalist aesthetic.

### Surface
*   **Canvas** (`{colors.canvas}` — #ffffff): The primary background color for the main content areas and the overall body.
*   **Surface Tertiary** (`{colors.surface-tertiary}` — #f5f5f7): A light gray used for backgrounds of certain sections, image cards, and the footer, providing subtle visual separation.
*   **Surface Global Nav Open** (`{colors.surface-globalnav-open}` — #fafafc): A very light, almost white, background used when the global navigation menu is opened.
*   **Surface Global Nav Open Dark** (`{colors.surface-globalnav-open-dark}` — #161617): A dark background for the global navigation when opened in a dark mode context (not explicitly observed on page, but defined).

### Text
*   **Text Primary** (`{colors.text-primary}` — #1d1d1f): The dominant dark gray color for most headings and body text, offering high contrast against light backgrounds.
*   **Text Secondary** (`{colors.text-secondary}` — #333336): A slightly lighter dark gray used for secondary text elements, such as search input placeholders.
*   **Text Muted** (`{colors.text-muted}` — #6e6e73): A medium gray used for less prominent text, such as helper text or secondary information.
*   **Text Footer Muted** (`{colors.text-footer-muted}` — rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.56)): A semi-transparent black used for text in the footer, providing a softer appearance.

## Typography

### Font Family
The primary typefaces used are "SF Pro Text" and "SF Pro Display", both part of Apple's San Francisco font family, known for its legibility and versatility across various screen sizes. "SF Pro Icons" is used for iconography. Fallback fonts include "Helvetica Neue", "Helvetica", "Arial", and generic "sans-serif" to ensure broad compatibility. For the footer, "Myriad Set Pro" and "Apple Legacy Chevron" are also listed as fallbacks.
