## Overview

The TealHQ website presents a modern, professional, and approachable visual identity, centered around empowering job seekers. The design leverages a clean aesthetic with a prominent brand blue, complemented by a range of accent colors. Typography is driven by the "Roobert" sans-serif typeface, providing clarity and a contemporary feel across various content hierarchies. Layouts are structured with generous vertical spacing and clear content segmentation, ensuring readability and ease of navigation, particularly on mobile. The overall impression is one of trustworthiness and efficiency, aiming to simplify the often-complex job search process for its users.

**Key Characteristics:**
*   **Primary Brand Blue**: `{colors.brand.primary}` — #5a6fec is a vibrant, consistent call-to-action and brand identifier.
*   **Clean, Modern Typography**: Exclusively uses "Roobert" sans-serif for all text, maintaining a cohesive and professional voice.
*   **Generous Vertical Spacing**: Sections are clearly delineated with ample padding, creating a breathable layout.
*   **Subtle Depth**: Minimal use of shadows adds a touch of modern elevation without being distracting.
*   **Rounded Corners**: A consistent application of `{rounded.base}` — 16px and `{rounded.lg}` — 34px softens UI elements.
*   **Mobile-First Layout**: Content is optimized for smaller screens with responsive padding and stacking.
*   **Functional Accent Colors**: A broad palette of accent colors is used sparingly for highlights and semantic cues.
*   **High Contrast Text**: Primary text is dark on light backgrounds, ensuring excellent legibility.
